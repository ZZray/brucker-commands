2. Temperature Commands

Temperature Commands

This chapter describes commands which configure, control or monitor the temperature unit. Temperature units which are delivered with Bruker spectrometers are often referred to as a eurotherm; the make of most units. They are used to warm up or cool down the NMR sample and keep it at a certain temperature during the experiment.

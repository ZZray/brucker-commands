2. Lock Commands

Lock Commands

This chapter describes the commands that are involved in locking the magnetic field. Avance spectrometers use a digital lock which is provided by the BSMS unit. Locking the magnetic field is done by measuring the signal of a lock nucleus and adjusting the magnetic field whenever its frequency shifts. Common lock nuclei are 2H and 19F.

NOTE
Please note that the Fourier 80 Lock procedures are described in the Fourier 80 User Manual.
